In this experiment you have to solve three different tasks. Each task will be played three times with three different incomes.

First Task:

You are given a fixed income.

Guess approximately how much you think you will spend on environmentally-friendly goods. Give a number in percent.

Second Task:

You are given a fixed income, which covers all the expenses you could possibly create. You have to choose between consumption goods out of five different areas.

For each consumption good you are given three choices with different prices.

Before each choice the differentiation in the goods will be explained.

Every expense you create will be subtracted from the fixed income.

You can distribute the income however you like, in the end every choice will be treated equally.
